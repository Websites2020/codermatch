How PushToStart works


PushToStart lets business owners in Washington, DC create projects and hire local freelancers. Have a start up idea? Use PushToStart to complete it. Got a new client requiring more of your resources, but don't have enough employees? Use PushToStart to hire a temporary work force.

Business Owners:

1) Post and manage all your projects from one account

2) Review proposals by local freelancers and choose the one that best matches your needs

Freelancers:

1) Search for projects posted by business owners

2) Log in to introduce yourself to businesses and get a paying project to work on
