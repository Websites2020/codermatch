How PushToStart works


PushToStart lets freelancers hire other freelancers to help complete their projects.

Writing a blog post and need research done? Use PushToStart to get it. Got a new client requiring more of your resources, but don't have enough time? Use PushToStart to hire a temporary work force.

Project leads:

1) Post and manage all your projects from one account

2) Review freelancers and choose the ones that best matches your needs

Freelancers:

1) Search for projects posted by project leads

2) Contact a project lead and start working on assignments
